Welcome

Start with a public link

1. Paste a public video or post link. The app works best with public links supported by yt-dlp, like YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X, and Reddit.
2. Click Preview. You can check the title, thumbnail, and available quality before saving anything.
3. Choose where to save it, then click Download. Most people only need local download mode. Advanced tunnel privacy options are optional.
